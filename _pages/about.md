I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Maryland, where I have the pleasure of being advised by [Professor Hajiaghayi](https://www.cs.umd.edu/~hajiagha). Before joining the University of Maryland, I received my B.Sc. in Computer Engineering at Sharif University of Technology in Tehran.

My research interests are broadly within the field of theoretical computer science, with a focus on approximation algorithms for graph and game theory problems.
